FAQ

What will I actually get?

3-4 validated MVP experiments, a landing page + outreach tested, 1:1 mentor reviews, and a 3-month roadmap to continue growth.

Will I have time to participate while keeping my main job?

Expect ~8-10 hours/week. We design each week for busy professionals: templates, timeboxing and peer accountability keep you moving without burning out.

Is this a "quit your job" program?

No - the program is about safe, structured transition. You keep your job while building a revenue stream and options.

How fast will I get first results?

If you stay committed, you can get your first paying clients or clear traction within 12 weeks. You'll leave with a strong direction and next steps.

Is there a result guarantee?

If you show up, do the homework and stay committed to the goal, we are confident you will get results. If you feel stuck, we offer you 1-1 coaching for free until you get your first clients.
